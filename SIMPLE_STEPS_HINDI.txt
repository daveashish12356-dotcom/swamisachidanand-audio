========================================
 DETAIL MEIN (agar upar samaj na aaye)
========================================

Kaam 2 (Release / audio files) baad me karo. Ab sirf ye:


--- Step 1 ---
Browser me ye link copy karke address bar me paste karo, Enter dabao:
https://github.com/daveashish12356-dotcom/swamisachidanand-audio


--- Step 2 ---
Page ke UPAR right side pe "Settings" likha hoga (gear icon ke saath).
Us "Settings" pe CLICK karo.


--- Step 3 ---
Left side list me sab options aayenge. Wahan "Pages" likha hoga.
Us "Pages" pe CLICK karo.


--- Step 4 ---
Ab page pe "Build and deployment" heading dikhegi.

Uske niche "Source" ke samne ek dropdown hoga.
Us dropdown me click karo aur "Deploy from a branch" CHOOSE karo.

Phir "Branch" ke samne dropdown me "main" CHOOSE karo.

Phir "Folder" ke samne dropdown me "/ (root)" CHOOSE karo.

Sab choose karne ke baad neeche "Save" button dikhega.
Us "Save" pe CLICK karo.


--- Step 5 ---
2 minute wait karo. Phir ye link kholo (copy-paste karo):
https://daveashish12356-dotcom.github.io/swamisachidanand-audio/audio_list.json

Agar kuch text (JSON) dikhe = ho gaya. App me audio list aa jayegi.
