========================================
 SIRF 2 KAAM - BILKUL SIMPLE
========================================

Code GitHub pe already push ho chuka hai. Ab sirf ye 2 cheezein karna hai.


■■■ KAAM 1: Pages ON karo ■■■

1. Browser me ye link kholo:
   https://github.com/daveashish12356-dotcom/swamisachidanand-audio

2. Page ke upar right side "Settings" likha hoga – uspe click karo.

3. Left side list me "Pages" dhundho – uspe click karo.

4. Neeche "Build and deployment" me:
   - Source: "Deploy from a branch" choose karo
   - Branch: "main" choose karo
   - Folder: "/ (root)" choose karo
   - "Save" button pe click karo

5. 2–3 minute wait karo. Phir ye link kholo:
   https://daveashish12356-dotcom.github.io/swamisachidanand-audio/audio_list.json

   Agar JSON text dikhe = list ready. App me audio list server se aayegi.


■■■ KAAM 2: Audio sunne ke liye – Release banao (baad me bhi kar sakte ho) ■■■

1. Wahi repo kholo: https://github.com/daveashish12356-dotcom/swamisachidanand-audio

2. "Releases" pe click karo (right side).

3. "Create a new release" pe click karo.

4. Tag me likho: amarakantak  →  "Create new tag" pe click karo.

5. Neeche "Publish release" pe click karo.

6. Release page pe "Attach binaries" – wahan 24 WAV files drag-drop karo.
   Names: part_01.wav, part_02.wav … part_24.wav
   Kaunsi file ka kaunsa naam – wo RELEASE_UPLOAD_MAP.txt me hai.

7. Upload ho jaye to save. Phir app me tap karte hi audio online chalegi.


========================================
 Short:
========================================
Kaam 1 (Pages) = app ko list milegi
Kaam 2 (Release + 24 WAV) = audio play hogi

Pehle Kaam 1 karo. Kaam 2 baad me bhi kar sakte ho.
